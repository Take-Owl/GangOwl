Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Overview

GangOwl ("we", "us", "our") operates the web-based gang sheet builder tool at gangowl.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

GangOwl does not collect personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require user accounts, sign-ups, or registration.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers.
  • We do not upload, store, or process your images on any server. All image processing happens entirely in your browser (client-side).
  • We do not sell or share any user data with third parties.

3. Local Storage

GangOwl uses your browser's localStorage to save your preferences and project data. This data:

  • Is stored entirely on your device.
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • Can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.
  • Does not contain personally identifiable information.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics that help us understand how visitors use our tool and improve the experience. Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site.
  • General geographic region (country/city level).
  • Browser type, device type, and screen resolution.
  • Referral sources (how you found the site).

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It does not identify individual users. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Google AdSense and Cookies

GangOwl displays advertisements through Google AdSense to support the free web version of the tool. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads. These third-party cookies may:

  • Track your browsing activity across websites to serve personalized advertisements.
  • Store preferences related to ad delivery.

We do not set any first-party cookies ourselves. All cookies on this site originate from third-party advertising services. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

6. Desktop Application

GangOwl also offers a desktop version sold through Gumroad. The desktop application does not collect any user data, does not communicate with external servers, and operates entirely offline on your machine.

7. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

GangOwl does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our service does not require any personal information to use.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through the contact information on our About page.
