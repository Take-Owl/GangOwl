About GangOwl

A free gang sheet builder for DTF printers, sublimation shops, vinyl cutters, and sticker makers.

What Is GangOwl?

GangOwl is a web-based gang sheet builder that lets you arrange multiple designs onto production-sized sheets for printing. You upload your artwork, set your sheet dimensions, and the tool handles layout, spacing, and export -- all in your browser with nothing to install.

The goal is straightforward: take a task that used to require manual Photoshop work or expensive RIP software and make it fast, free, and accessible to anyone with a web browser.

Who It Is For

GangOwl is built for anyone who needs to fit multiple designs onto a single sheet:

  • DTF printers -- arrange transfers on DTF film rolls to maximize coverage and reduce waste.
  • Sublimation shops -- lay out mugs, tumblers, and apparel designs on sublimation paper.
  • Vinyl cutters -- pack heat transfer vinyl (HTV) designs efficiently before cutting.
  • Sticker makers -- gang sheet sticker designs with die-cut contour lines for print-and-cut workflows.
  • Small business owners -- anyone doing short-run custom printing who needs to keep material costs low.

Key Features

Auto-Place

Drag and drop your images, or let the auto-place engine arrange them to fill the sheet with minimal waste.

Smart Guides

Alignment guides and snap-to-edge behavior help you position designs precisely without guesswork.

Multi-Sheet

When designs overflow one sheet, GangOwl distributes them across multiple sheets automatically.

Die-Cut Contours

Generate automatic contour cut lines around each design for print-and-cut sticker production.

Export as PNG / PDF

Export production-ready sheets at full resolution. PDF export supports multi-page output for multi-sheet jobs.

Browser-Based

Everything runs locally in your browser. No account, no upload to a server, no waiting.

Why GangOwl Exists

Gang sheet layout is a solved problem in large-scale print shops -- their RIP software handles it. But for small businesses and individuals doing short-run DTF, sublimation, or sticker work, the options have historically been limited to manual layout in Photoshop or paying for software that costs more than the printer itself.

GangOwl was built to close that gap. The idea is simple: a focused tool that does one thing well -- arranging designs on sheets -- without the overhead of a full design suite. It should be free to use, fast to load, and require zero setup. Upload your images, set your sheet size, and export. That is it.
